About Amity University
Amity University, established by the State Legislature and recognized by the UGC, stands as India's leading private education institution. Recognized among Asia's top 200 universities by QS and UK rankings, it is the sole Indian university accredited by the WASQ, US. With campuses worldwide, including Dubai, Abu Dhabi, London, Singapore, and New York, offering 300+ UG and PG programs and boasting a faculty of 6,000, we are a coveted global education hub with 150,000 students.
Our Vision:
We're dedicated to providing top-quality education to people from all walks of life, fostering success and growth. Our goal is to empower individuals from diverse backgrounds through exceptional online education that makes a lasting impact.
Our Mission:
Our mission is to offer comprehensive education across diverse disciplines, including emerging fields, to nurture excellence in both professional and personal aspects. We aim to mold students into compassionate individuals with strong ethics, cultural pride, and a pursuit of perfection.
