Eligibility Criteria

  • Fresh graduates and working professionals.

  • Entrepreneurs looking to develop skills to manage critical business projects.

  • Aspirants or working professionals seeking a global career in the fields of Management, Finance, Marketing, Sales, Operations, Logistics among many more.

For Indian students

  • Graduation in any discipline with 40% marks in the last qualifying examination. Below 40% marks, a test is conducted and if passed then the student is eligible for admission.

  • Applicants must possess sufficient knowledge and understanding of the English Language.

For Foreign students

  • Candidates must have completed a graduation degree

  • Certificate of equivalence from the Association of Indian Universities required from students with foreign education to apply to any university in India. (Refer to http://aiu.ac.in/)

Yes, an online MBA is a degree that offers flexibility to students along with a good learning experience and a high-quality curriculum. MBA Online Courses are generally interactive and offer excellent scope in terms of career and quality education to students who have other responsibilities and can’t attend regular classes.

What career opportunities are available with an Online MBA courses?

+

An Online MBA Course opens avenues to careers in different fields for graduates. A skilled MBA online graduate can work as a management consultant, HR manager, Marketing manager, IT personnel, Healthcare manager, and entrepreneur, among other positions.

How does Amity Online’s MBA program compare to other top programs?

+

The online MBA degree offered by Amity Online is one of the most popular Online MBA Courses in India. This program is recognized by the University Grants Commission Distance Education Bureau (UGC-DEB). Amity Online MBA program has been accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade. This program is regarded as one of the best programs offered by Amity Online.

What are the benefits of pursuing an online MBA?

+

An Online MBA allows students to be flexible and help them to complete their studies at their own pace. Hence, Online MBA Courses suits working professionals and students who are performing other roles including jobs to support their finances. These courses have reasonable fees as compared to regular MBA programs. They allow students networking opportunities and excellent job and placement opportunities.

Can the Online MBA program be completed while working full-time?

+

Yes, with online MBA programs, candidates can pursue part-time or full-time jobs. This is because online MBAs do not demand students to attend regular and in-person classes. They allow students the flexibility to balance their studies with their work and family.
