The admission process is simple:
  • Select your program and complete the online application form.
  • Upload the required documents and pay the registration fee.
  • Upon acceptance, confirm your enrollment by making the program fee payment.

Admission requirements vary by program:
  • For undergraduate programs: Completion of 10+2.
  • For postgraduate programs: A bachelor's degree is required.
  • Academic certificates and a valid government-issued ID.
  • Some programs may require additional eligibility tests.

Admissions are conducted twice annually. Updates and deadlines are posted on the official website.

After completing the enrollment process, students receive a confirmation email with login credentials for the E-Campus portal, granting access to learning materials and resources.

For a smooth admission process, the following documents are required:
For Domestic Students:
  • Academic certificates from 10th, 12th, and graduation.
  • Consolidated marksheets for graduation.
  • A valid government-issued ID such as Aadhar, PAN, or Passport.
For International Students:
  • O Level and A Level certificates or equivalent qualifications.
