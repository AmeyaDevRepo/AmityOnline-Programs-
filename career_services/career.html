Meet our top-ranked faculty

Learn from the best at Amity Online with world-class
education and leading International faculty

Neha Tandon

Assistant Professor,
8+ years of experience; & Double PG in Management & Commerce

Neha Tandon is an Assistant Professor at Amity University, Noida, with over 3 years of experience in this role. She has a strong background in International Business, Accounting, Managerial Finance, and content creation for e-learning. Neha has also worked as an Assistant Professor at the Institute of Management Studies, Noida. She holds an MBA in Finance from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University and a B.Sc. (Honors) from Delhi University.

Dr. Sunil Kumar

Assistant Professor,
10+ years of experience; P.hD. in Management

Sunil is an assistant professor also he holds 11 years of academic experience, with Pune Institute of Business Management and Galgotias University, and he has a Ph.D. in Management. He has worked in supply chain management and quality control for Pepsi Foods and Calpro Foods. In addition, Dr. Kumar is the author of multiple scientific publications, with published patents in India, and an invention patent from IP Australia.

Sachit Paliwal

Assistant Professor,
12+ years of experience; MBA in Finance Management

Sachit Paliwal is a seasoned academician with 14 years of experience in teaching and academic administration. He holds a Ph.D. in Finance, an MBA in Finance and is UGC-NET qualified in both Commerce and Management. Currently, he serves as Assistant Professor and Course Coordinator at Amity University Online, managing accounting and finance programs, curriculum development, and e-learning platforms.

Dr Ron Darnell

International Faculty,
Ph.D. from Capella University; MBA from University of Texas

Ron, is an Agile Enablement Coach and University Professor who has vast experience with Agile transformations and project management. With over 20 years of academic experience, he also advises corporations like Verizon, Sony Pictures, and Southwest Airlines, helping them navigate Agile transitions. His academic credentials include a Ph.D. in Organisation Management, an MBA in MIS, and a B.S. in Economics & Finance.

Dr. Hayley Stainton

International Faculty,
11+ years of experience; Ph.D. from Coventry University

Dr. Hayley Stainton is an international school leader, academic, and founder of Tourism Teacher, with expertise in geography and tourism. She has extensive teaching experience at various levels, from secondary to postgraduate, and is a Chair of Examiners for Pearson. She has held roles such as Assistant Headteacher at NCBIS and Senior Lecturer at Buckinghamshire New University.

Luke Pearce

International Faculty,
10+ years of experience; Master's in Education and Language

Luke is an English teacher, associate lecturer, and writer with more than ten years of experience in education. He has a Master's degree in Education and Languages, a PGCE in Post-16 and Further Education, a CELTA certification, and a BA in Politics and Spanish. His teaching experience includes UCL, EFL, EAP, ESOL, GCSE, etc.
